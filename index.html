About Me

About me

Hey, I'm Kai, also known as Hassnain!

I love building digital worlds and environments that feel real and tell stories. There's something really satisfying about crafting spaces that people want to explore.

I work mainly in Blender, spending most of my time getting the lighting and materials just right. Good composition is everything when you're trying to make an environment that actually feels alive.

I'm constantly learning new ways to make my environments better and more engaging for whatever project I'm working on.
