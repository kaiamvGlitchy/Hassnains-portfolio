About Me

Hey there, I'm Kai also known as Hassnain, im a begginer 3d artist, i started blender around 6 months ago, im still learning and exploring the vast world of 3d art, i will share my journey and progress here.

I believe a great render should tell a story, not just showcase technical skills and I'm always looking for new ways to tell them. I'm always eager to collaborate on new projects, so if you have a creative vision you'd like to bring to life, don't hesitate to reach out!

My core workflow is built around the industry-standard software below, but I'm always learning and experimenting with new tools to improve my craft and efficiency.

  • 3D Modeling: Blender, Maya, ZBrush
  • Texturing: Substance Painter, Substance Designer
  • Rendering: Cycles, Eevee, Arnold, V-Ray
  • Post-Production: Adobe Photoshop, After Effects, DaVinci Resolve

My artistic philosophy is centered on the idea that every piece should have a purpose. Whether it's to evoke a specific emotion, tell a story, or simply highlight a product's elegance, the intent behind the art is what makes it powerful.

I strive for a balance between technical precision and creative expression, ensuring that the final output is not only beautiful but also technically sound and efficient. My goal is to create work that inspires and captivates, pushing the boundaries of what's possible in the digital realm.
